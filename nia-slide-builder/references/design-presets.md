# Design Presets

## Design 01: Clean SaaS

Default design. Use for company education, AI/workflow topics, internal presentations, SaaS product concepts, and polished business decks.

Visual traits:

- White slide canvas on a cool gray page background.
- Large bold Korean headings, usually `48px` to `64px`.
- Blue tag chips, key phrase accents, and selected cards.
- Rounded cards between `16px` and `24px`.
- Soft shadows and subtle borders.
- Layouts built from two-column grids, three-step cards, callout cards, and comparison blocks.

Best slide types:

- Title thesis slide.
- Three-stage process.
- Before/after comparison.
- Workflow loop.
- Key takeaway with supporting cards.

## Design 02: Executive Brief

Use for leadership, strategy, KPI, market analysis, financial summaries, and board-style updates.

Visual traits:

- More neutral surfaces and fewer saturated fills.
- Smaller title scale, usually `36px` to `48px`.
- Dense but organized tables, metric rows, and charts.
- Thin dividers, restrained tags, and strong alignment.
- Blue used mainly for highlights and selected numbers.

Best slide types:

- KPI dashboard.
- Decision memo.
- Risk/opportunity matrix.
- Quarterly summary.
- Roadmap or investment tradeoff.

## Design 03: Workshop Playbook

Use for training, lectures, exercises, onboarding, and hands-on enablement.

Visual traits:

- Clear step labels and instructional modules.
- More visible sectioning than Executive Brief.
- Checklists, practice prompts, role cards, and example boxes.
- Friendly but still professional tone.
- Blue primary actions with small success/warning accents for feedback.

Best slide types:

- Lesson agenda.
- Exercise instructions.
- Checklist.
- Example and practice pair.
- Reflection or recap.

## Selection Rule

If the user says:

- "깔끔하게", "회사 발표", "토스 같은", "SaaS 느낌": choose `Design 01`.
- "임원 보고", "전략", "KPI", "투자자": choose `Design 02`.
- "강의", "워크숍", "교육", "실습": choose `Design 03`.

## User-Facing Choice Labels

Use these short labels when asking the user to choose:

- `Design 01 Clean SaaS`: 깔끔한 회사 발표/AI 업무 자료.
- `Design 02 Executive Brief`: 임원 보고/전략/KPI 중심 자료.
- `Design 03 Workshop Playbook`: 강의/교육/실습 중심 자료.
