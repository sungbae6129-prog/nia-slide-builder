# NIA Brand Assets

Use only the assets bundled under `assets/nia-brand/`.

## Official CI

| File | Use |
|---|---|
| `nia-ci-horizontal-ko.png` | Official horizontal Korean NIA CI. Required on cover and closing slides. |

## Transparent Nyanya PNG Selection

All Nyanya files have transparent backgrounds. They can be placed directly on white, colored, gradient, or image backgrounds without a visible rectangular box.

| File | Recommended use |
|---|---|
| `nyanya-default.png` | Default cover choice. Neutral, confident, technology-oriented. |
| `nyanya-fullbody-1.png` | Technology, AI, digital strategy, research, or system architecture. |
| `nyanya-fullbody-3.png` | Energetic innovation, campaigns, achievements, or momentum. |
| `nyanya-variation-2.png` | Celebration, positive results, encouragement, or closing alternative. |
| `nyanya-variation-4.png` | Warm smile and friendly general communication. |
| `nyanya-variation-7.png` | Appreciation and thank-you closing. Preferred closing choice. |

## Selection Logic

1. Default cover: `nyanya-default.png`.
2. Technology, AI, or research cover: prefer `nyanya-fullbody-1.png`.
3. Innovation, achievement, or campaign cover: prefer `nyanya-fullbody-3.png`.
4. Friendly general cover: prefer `nyanya-default.png` or `nyanya-variation-4.png`.
5. Default closing: `nyanya-variation-7.png`.
6. Celebratory closing: `nyanya-variation-2.png`.

## Placement

- Cover Nyanya: typically right side, 28-40% of slide width.
- Cover CI: top-left or bottom-left, 18-28% of slide width.
- Closing Nyanya: centered or slightly above center, 22-32% of slide width.
- Closing CI: horizontally centered near the bottom, 18-24% of slide width.
- Preserve transparent padding only as needed; use `object-fit: contain`.
- Keep clear space around the CI and do not overlap it with other elements.
